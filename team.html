Meet Our Team

Becca Henry

Becca Henry is a Board Certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner, proficient in a myriad of mental health treatment modalities and experienced in treating a broad spectrum of symptoms and diagnoses across all age groups.

Starting her career as a Registered Nurse at Johns Hopkins Hospital's Child and Adolescent Behavioral Health Unit, she later moved to Oregon after obtaining her advanced practice licensure. Her career trajectory spans diverse settings, such as private practice, residential treatment, community health, detoxification, addiction recovery, and crisis intervention, thereby cultivating a philosophy centered on holistic care.

  • Becca believes in the necessity of treating the mind and body together and uses a variety of treatment options to maximize healthy outcomes.
Laura Tamkin

Laura has an MA in psychology. She has worked in various settings as a mental health therapist and as an art therapist. She currently employs brief solution based interventions. Laura sees clients of all ages and is currently one of our three Neurofeedback clinician.

Cara Chapman
(PMHNP)

Cara is a board certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner with an extensive background in the treatment of mental health conditions. She is an experienced psychiatric prescriber as well as a psychotherapist. It is Cara's belief that a strong therapeutic alliance with clients leads to more successful outcomes. Cara has experience in a variety of psychiatric settings including outpatient, partial hospitalization, and acute care. She has worked in the outpatient setting for the past 19 years, because she enjoys developing therapeutic relationships with her clients. Cara also believes that it is important to collaborate with other providers that work with the client in order to provide holistic and comprehensive care.

  • Cara cultivates strong therapeutic alliances and emphasizes collaboration in mental health care, utilizing her skills in psychotherapy and psychiatric prescribing to promote holistic healing.

Bob Lee

Robert specializes in case management and provides multidisciplinary Care Coordination and Medical Advocacy for complex cases. Robert is also one of our Neurofeedback clinicians. Robert is compassion-driven. A survivor of the Southeast, Robert transplanted to Oregon many years ago. He is an outdoor and whitewater enthusiast.

  • Robert blends expertise in case management, neurofeedback, and medical advocacy, offering compassionate, multidisciplinary care tailored to complex needs.

Ian Shinney

Dr. Ian Shinney is a licensed psychologist in Oregon specializing in client-centered mental health treatment. Proficient in a wide array of evidence-based modalities, he provides therapeutic support across the lifespan, with a particular focus and extensive experience in treating children and adolescents (ages 0–18).
A native of Oregon, Dr. Shinney received his education and training in Chicago and the Bay Area. His diverse experience includes work in pediatric hospitals, residential treatment centers, community mental health clinics, and school settings. He has been providing dedicated therapeutic services in the Eugene area since 2009.
Dr. Shinney approaches therapy as a collaborative journey, partnering with clients to provide guidance, critical insight, and support that addresses the holistic person and fosters lasting well-being.

Hillary Halfmann

Lucia
(Office Administrator)

Lucia is a friendly face who greets each client at Beautiful Minds. As the front office administrator, she takes pride in creating a welcoming and supportive environment where clients feel seen, heard and cared for. She is known for her warm personality, attention to detail and making clients feel at ease. Whether managing appointments, coordinating with clinicians or assisting with other administrative needs, she ensures that each interaction reflects the care and compassion that define our mission here at Beautiful Minds. Outside of the office, she enjoys jewelry making, spending time outdoors and connecting with friends and family.

Kevin

Kevin is a Eugene native who held residency at the Charnelton office space prior to the Beautiful Minds move to this location. With much charm and persistence, Kevin managed to secure a full-time position as the official Ambassador of Beautiful Minds Mental Health and Wellness Center. When Kevin is not busy greeting clients and ensuring they receive the highest level of care; he enjoys a frolic in the garden, nap in the lobby when the sun is just right and well-earned tuna treats.
