Online Privacy Policy Agreement

August 26, 2024

Beautiful Minds Mental Health and Wellness Center (“Beautiful Minds”) values its users privacy. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) will help you understand how we collect and use personal information from those who visit our website or make use of our online facilities and services. Our Policy has been designed and created to ensure those affiliated with Beautiful Minds of our commitment to our obligations not only to meet, but to exceed privacy standards.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Policy at any given time. If you want to make sure that you are up to date with the latest changes, please check back to this page frequently. If at any point Beautiful Minds decides to make use of any personal identifiable information on file greatly different than initially stated, we will swiftly notify the effected users via email. At that time said users will have the option as to whether or not they permit the use of their information in said stated manner.

This Policy applies to Beautiful Minds. Through the use of www.beautifulmindsmhwc.org and text communications at 541-777-8805, you are consenting to the data procedures expressed in this Policy.

Please note that this Policy does not govern the collection and misuse of information by companies other than Beautiful Minds, nor by employees or individuals employed or managed by us. If you visit a website we mention, suggest or offer a link to, be sure to review their privacy policy before providing the site with information. It is highly recommended that you review the privacy policies of any website you choose to use or frequent to better understand the information shared and collected.

Information we collect

It is always up to the patient whether you choose to disclose personally identifiable information, although should you choose not to do so, we reserve the right to not register you as a patient and provide you with services. As a clinic we collect various types of information, such as:

● Legal name, address, email address, phone number, billing and/or credit card information etc. which would be used only upon consent upon signing consent or initial outreach on behalf of yourself or another patient.
In addition Beautiful Minds may collect other demographic information, such as age, gender, gender identity, race, i.e. We will only collect personal information through a secured patient portal that you knowingly and willingly provide to us via completed, signed and dated new patient documents. It is the intent of this practice to use personal information only for the purpose for which it was requested and any additional reasons specifically provided in this Policy.

Why We Collect Information

● To better understand your needs and provide you with services requested and deemed necessary.
● To contact you to fill out intake documents, schedule appointments, send referrals and provide you with the best possible personalized care.
The information we collect is used only for the time period necessary for your care. The length of time we retain said information is determined by the following criteria: the length of time your personal information remains relevant; the length of time it is reasonable to keep records to demonstrate that we have fulfilled our requirements and duties; any retention periods determined by law or recommended by regulations or associations; and the existence of your written consent.

Disclosure of Information

Beautiful Minds does not now, nor will it ever sell, rent, lease or share any of its patient lists and/or names, numbers or any personal demographics to ANY third parties.

Beautiful Minds may not use or disclose the information provided by you except under the following circumstances:

● As necessary to provide services/treatment you have requested
● In other ways described in this Policy or to which you have given written consent
● As required by law, or in response to a subpoena or search warrant
● As necessary to enforce the terms of service.
Unsubscribe or Opt-Out

All potential and current patients have the option to opt out of email, text and voicemail communications. To unsubscribe or opt-out from messages please send an email to sarah.floyd@beautifulmindsmhwc.org or reply STOP to 541-777-8805. If you want to unsubscribe or opt-out of any third-party websites you must visit that specific website to unsubscribe. Beautiful Minds will continue to adhere to this Policy with respect to your specific means of communication.

Security

Beautiful Minds uses HIPPA compliant means of communication whether over the phone, via text or communications through the patient portal. When you submit information via the portal your information is protected both online and offline. When collecting credit card information that information is encrypted and transmitted in a secure way. You can verify this by looking for a lock icon in the address bar and looking for “https” at the beginning of the address of the webpage.

While we use encryption to protect sensitive information online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to provide you direct care are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers and servers in which we store personal information are kept in secure environments and are password protected. This is all done to prevent any loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure or modification of the patient’s personal information.

Acceptance of Terms and Conditions

By using the patient portal and communicating via text you are hereby accepting the terms and conditions stipulated withing this Privacy Policy Agreement. If you are not in agreement with our terms and conditions, then you should refrain from further use as well as not share any personal information or opt out from further communication. In addition, your continued use of our websites and communication means following any updates or changes to our terms and conditions shall be an automatic acceptance of such changes.
